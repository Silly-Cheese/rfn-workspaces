RFN Authority Model

Observe. Act. Direct. Own.

The platform is built around a clear authority chain. Staff can observe assigned work, management can act, senior leadership can direct, and ownership retains final system control.

ObserveStaff can view assigned workspaces, tasks, and tickets.
ActManagement can update records, respond to tickets, and handle operations.
DirectSenior management and executives can review, escalate, and approve actions.
OwnThe CEO controls platform direction, access, and final administrative authority.
